# Body Speaks — Vercel Deployment Instructions

## Folder structure you're deploying:
bodyspeaks-vercel/
├── api/
│   └── create-customer.js   ← serverless function (Square token lives here)
├── public/
│   └── index.html           ← the tool
└── vercel.json              ← routing config

---

## STEP 1 — Deploy to Vercel

1. Go to vercel.com → Sign up or log in (free)
2. Click "Add New Project"
3. Choose "Upload" (drag and drop the entire `bodyspeaks-vercel` FOLDER)
   - Or connect your GitHub if you have the folder there
4. Click Deploy — it will give you a `.vercel.app` URL

---

## STEP 2 — Add Environment Variables (CRITICAL — do this before testing)

In Vercel → your project → Settings → Environment Variables, add:

| Name                  | Value                                          |
|-----------------------|------------------------------------------------|
| SQUARE_ACCESS_TOKEN   | [Your Production Access Token from Square]     |
| SQUARE_LOCATION_ID    | LZ99A2XXZJ2F1                                  |

⚠️ IMPORTANT: After adding your env variables, go to Deployments → click the three dots on your latest deployment → "Redeploy" so the variables take effect.

---

## STEP 3 — Add Custom Domain

In Vercel → your project → Settings → Domains:
Add: bodyspeaks.icu-1111.com

Vercel will show you the exact CNAME value to use.

---

## STEP 4 — Add CNAME in your DNS

Log into wherever icu-1111.com DNS is managed:
- Type: CNAME
- Name: bodyspeaks
- Value: [whatever Vercel gives you — usually cname.vercel-dns.com]

DNS propagation takes 5–60 minutes.

---

## STEP 5 — Regenerate your Square token

Since your token was visible in Claude chat, regenerate it:
1. developer.squareup.com → Credentials → Production
2. Click "Regenerate" next to Production Access Token
3. Copy the NEW token
4. Update the SQUARE_ACCESS_TOKEN env variable in Vercel
5. Redeploy

---

## What happens when someone completes the tool:
- They enter name + email on the capture screen
- They click "Reveal My Pattern Analysis"
- Their info is sent to /api/create-customer (server-side, token never exposed)
- A new customer is created in your Square account
- They are added to the "ICU-1111" customer group
- Their dominant PAST pattern + all selected conditions are saved in the customer note
- A green toast notification confirms "✓ Your results have been saved"

If they skip the email step, they still see results — Square just doesn't receive anything.
